Take Control of your online identity

The Privacy Stick features a plug-and-play preconfigured operating system based on The Amnesic Incognito Live System to encrypt your network activity and greatly reduce vulernability of oustsiders accessing your sensitive data.

The Amnesic Incognito Live System uses onion routing (layered encryption) which was originally created by the U.S. Navy to protect government communications. Every session hosted by the operating system is erased at shut-down without touching your hard drive, leaving no trace behind.

Each profile includes 6 GB of internal storage for files that may need to be saved within the flash drive   (can be paired with external storage)

Three profiles to choose from for each session

Student Privacy Anonymous

Student Profile

 

Optimized for the user who wants to encrypt their data in public without standing out. The student profile is a Windows 10 look alike with the option of using a non-encrypted browser for sites that may block use of encrypted networks.

Anonymous Profile

 

Every session using the anonymous profile is encrypted and erased completely at shutdown. There are no configurements or ability to save files through to the next session, making this profile the least identifiable and most secure.

Privacy Profile

 

The standard profile, configured for ease of use. The privacy profile can only use the encrypted browser keeping your online fingerprint hidden at all times, while still giving users the ability to save files in Internal Storage.

* System Requirements: 64 bit processor, 2 GB RAM, Linux Compatible GPU

You don't have to have something to hide to value your privacy.

     

Online freedom comes from a person feeling safe and posessing the ability to set their own boundaries when it comes to the data they share. With our devices constantly tracking and collecting data about relationships, social media use, videos we watch, banking information, etc. they have become a window into our personal lives.

It is my belief that industry and government around the world should work even more closely to protect the privacy and security of Internet users, and promote the exchange of ideas, while respecting legitimate government considerations. - Bill Gates

Privacy Stick aims to provide internet users around world with the option to choose what online information they would like to keep truly private, and reduce vulnerability of that information being accessed by somebody with malicious intent. Every individual deserves the right to keep their data away from peering eyes; keeping sensitive data encrypted and seperate from your online fingerprint is a layer of security towards your online risks.

We have been fed the narrative that hiding your online identity means you have dark interests, or are connected to criminal activity. This a dangerous way to move forward in a world where technology is becoming ever more prominent in our daily routines and way of living. The amount of information about ourselves we are putting online is increasing, and so are cyber crimes with there being a 158% increase of ransomware attacks between 2019 and 2020 in North America alone. Not everybody is looking out for best interests and these are the people that we need to protect our private data and inside lives from.
